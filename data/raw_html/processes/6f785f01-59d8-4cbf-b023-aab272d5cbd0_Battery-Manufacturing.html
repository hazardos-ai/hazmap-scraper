Battery Manufacturing

Process Name
Battery Manufacturing
Description
Potential exposures in battery manufacturing include: 1) lead-acid batteries--lead and sulfuric acid; 2) nickel-cadmium batteries--nickel and cadmium; 3) dry-cell batteries--manganese, zinc, and mercury; 4) mercury cell--mercury and potassium hydroxide; [Burgess, p. 401-408] See "Arsenic exposures during burning activities in battery assembly." Appl Occup Environ Hyg. 2000 Sep;15(9):664-6.
Category
Industry
