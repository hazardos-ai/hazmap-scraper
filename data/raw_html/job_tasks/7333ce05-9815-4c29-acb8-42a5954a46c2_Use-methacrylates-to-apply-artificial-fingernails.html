Use methacrylates to apply artificial fingernails

Job Task Name
Use methacrylates to apply artificial fingernails
Comments
Occupational asthma caused by ethyl methacrylate has been reported. [Malo] See "Perceived work exposures and expressed intervention needs among Michigan nail salon workers." [PMID 34052870] See "VOC sources and exposures in nail salons: a pilot study in Michigan, USA." [PMID 30276513]
