Spray polyurethane foam insulation

Job Task Name
Spray polyurethane foam insulation
Comments
Polyurethane (TDI, mainly for flexible foams and MDI, mainly for rigid foams) can cause occupational asthma and hypersensitivity pneumonitis. See "Preventing Asthma and Death from Diisocyanate Exposure" at http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/asthma.html?
Job Task Category
Installing or Removing Insulation
