Use solder containing colophony, zinc chloride, ethanolamine, or ammonium chloride flux

Job Task Name
Use solder containing colophony, zinc chloride, ethanolamine, or ammonium chloride flux
Comments
Occupational asthma has been reported in 1) electronics workers soldering with colophony flux; 2) workers manufacturing rosin core solder; 3) metal jointers using zinc chloride and ammonium chloride flux; and, 4) electronic assemblers using flux containing 95% polyether alcohol + 5% polypropylene glycol. Asthma also confirmed in a solderer using adipic acid and N-(2-Hydroxyethyl)ethylenediamine; [Malo]
Job Task Category
Heating, Machining, or Welding Metal
Exposed To
Allergens
