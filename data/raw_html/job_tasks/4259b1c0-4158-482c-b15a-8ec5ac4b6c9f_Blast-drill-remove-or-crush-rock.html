Blast, drill, remove, or crush rock

Job Task Name
Blast, drill, remove, or crush rock
Comments
High-risk jobs for metal and non-metal miners are stone polishers/cutters, ball mill operators, rotary drill operators, and crusher operators. Most jobs drilling, cutting, or breaking rock had average exposures >50 ug/m3 plus likely exposure to freshly fractured quartz. [Hyperlink]
