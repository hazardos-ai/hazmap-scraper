Arc weld aluminum

Job Task Name
Arc weld aluminum
Comments
Pneumonitis, acute; Bronchitis, chronic; Arc welding aluminum may produce high concentrations of ozone which can cause acute inhalation injury and chronic bronchitis. Aluminum alloys are usually joined by MIG or TIG welding. The filler alloy is the major contributor to the particulate in the welding fume.
