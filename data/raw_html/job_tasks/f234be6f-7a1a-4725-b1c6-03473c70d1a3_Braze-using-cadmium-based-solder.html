Braze using cadmium-based solder

Job Task Name
Braze using cadmium-based solder
Comments
Jewelers using silver solder and workers soldering automotive radiators are at risk for cadmium poisoning (chronic exposure) and acute pneumonitis (acute, heavy exposure).
Job Task Category
Heating, Machining, or Welding Metal
Exposed To
Metals
