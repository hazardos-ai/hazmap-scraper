Category
Azo Dyes
Description
Bright orange-yellow solid; [Merck Index] Hygroscopic; [HSDB] Orange powder; [MSDSonline]
Sources/Uses
Used as dye for wool, silk, food, drugs, cosmetics, and pesticides; Used in biochemistry (indicator) and in sanitizing solutions for food-processing equipment, utensils, and other items that contact food; [HSDB]
Comments
An allergen; [Hawley] Its use in coloring oral medications has caused allergic reactions in small percentage of patients; In one study of 2210 patients exposed to tartrazine in drugs, 3.8% had allergic reactions that subsided within 48 hours; There were no allergies to drugs not containing tartrazine; No significant adverse effects were observed in rats during long term feeding studies; Intraperitoneal LD50 (rat) >2000 mg/kg; [HSDB] An irritant; May cause skin and respiratory sensitization after prolonged contact; [MSDSonline]
