Category
Organic Acids, Metal Salts
Description
White odorless solid; Slightly hygroscopic; [HSDB]
Sources/Uses
Used as an antidote in metal poisoning, a sequestering agent in foods, an in vitro anticoagulant of blood, and an antigushing agent in beer; Also used in drugs, cosmetics, and other chemicals to sequester trace minerals and prevent oxidation and discoloration; [HSDB]
Comments
Accidental administration of five times the normal dose, infused over 24 hours, caused no adverse effects to a 16 month old child being treated for lead poisoning; Repeated large doses cause degeneration of the proximal tubular cells of the kidneys; See possible adverse effects to patient when used as an intravenous drug; [HSDB] An irritant; [MSDSonline] See "Edetic acid."
