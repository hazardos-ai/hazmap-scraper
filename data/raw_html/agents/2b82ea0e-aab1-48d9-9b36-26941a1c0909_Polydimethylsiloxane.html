Polydimethylsiloxane

Polydimethylsiloxane

9016-00-6; 63148-62-9

(C2-H6-O-Si)x-

Category
Polymers
Description
Colorless liquid; [ICSC] Colorless odorless viscous liquid; [MSDSonline]
Sources/Uses
Used as a water-repellent, a lubricant, a defoaming agent in oil processing, a flow/gloss improver for paints and varnishes, and a sizing agent for textiles and paper; Also used in wound dressing, in soft contact lenses, in fire resistant transformers, in hydraulic/dielectric/heat-transfer/diffusion pump oils, and in silicone products; [HSDB] Used as a mold-release agent, an anti-caking agent for powdered foods, and for medical applications such as tubing and implants; [CHEMINFO]
Comments
Emergency treatment: "Silicones"; [HSDB] An eye irritant; [ICSC]; May cause irritation; [MSDSonline] See "Polydimethylsiloxane rubber."
