3,4,5,3',4'-Pentachlorobiphenyl

Agent Name
3,4,5,3',4'-Pentachlorobiphenyl
CAS Number
57465-28-8
Formula
C12-H5-Cl5
Major Category
Other Classes
Synonyms
3,3',4,4',5-Pentachloro-1,1'-biphenyl; 3,3',4,4',5-Pentachlorobiphenyl; 3,4,3',4',5'-Pentachlorobiphenyl; 3,4,5,3',4'-Penta coplanar polychlorinated biphenyl; PCB 126; 1,1'-Biphenyl, 3,3',4,4',5-pentachloro-; [ChemIDplus] UN3432
Category
Halogenated Polyaromatics
Description
White solid; [Cerilliant MSDS]
Comments
Clear evidence of carcinogenicity in female rats; [NTP] Classified as Group 1 based on animal evidence and mechanistic data (strong evidence for an aryl hydrocarbon receptor) without epidemiological evidence; [IARC 2012] May have cumulative adverse effects; Targets the liver, kidney, CNS, and skin; [Cerilliant MSDS] See "Polychlorinated biphenyls" and linked occupational disease.
