Description
Colorless to white solid; [ICSC]
Sources/Uses

Comments
Ingestion of large amounts may induce methemoglobinemia. [HSDB] May explode on heating, shock, friction, or concussion; May cause lowering of blood pressure; [ICSC] A high explosive and strong oxidizing agent; [CAMEO]
