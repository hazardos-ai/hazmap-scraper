Woodworking

Activity Name
Woodworking
Description
Potential exposure to arsenic, formaldehyde, pentachlorophenol, creosote, solvents (methylene chloride, Stoddard, toluene, turpentine), glues (epoxy resin, formaldehyde resin, cyanoacrylate), and wood dusts including many that have caused both allergic contact dermatitis and asthma, e.g. iroko, ebony, western red cedar, California redwood, and ramin; [Rom, 3rd ed, p. 1467] [ILO Encyclo, Vol. 3, p. 96.1-96.54] Some of the woods that most commonly cause skin and respiratory sensitization are listed. [www.ci.tucson.az.us/arthazards/medium.html]
